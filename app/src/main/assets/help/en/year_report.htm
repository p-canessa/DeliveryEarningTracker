Annual Summary

The annual summary screen allows you to view your earnings statistics on an annual basis. This report is useful both for tax payments for freelancers and for monitoring the 5,000 â‚¬ maximum threshold for occasional workers. You can access this view by selecting the "Annual Summary" option from the side menu.

Report Period

The annual report is calculated "on a cash basis," covering the period from December of the previous year to November of the selected year. This is because payments are made the following month (e.g., for the year 2025, the report includes data from December 2024 to November 2025).

Screen Details

The screen consists of the following main elements:

  • Dropdown Menu: At the top, there is a dropdown menu that allows you to select the desired year (e.g., 2024, 2025, etc.).
  • Data Table: A table summarizes your earnings with the following columns and rows:
    • Columns: Category, Gross, Net, VAT.
    • Rows: Orders, Adjustments, Tips, Total.
  • Delivery Information: Below the table, the following is indicated:
    • The number of deliveries made during the year.
    • The number of deliveries remaining to reach the bonus (Italian riders receive a bonus every 2,000 deliveries).

How to Use the Annual Report

  • Ensure you have entered orders and imported relevant paystubs (see Manual Entry, OCR Entry, and Paystub Import).
  • Select the desired year from the dropdown menu to view the corresponding data.
  • Analyze the values in the table to understand your gross earnings, net earnings, and associated VAT for each category (orders, adjustments, tips) and the overall total.
  • Check the number of deliveries made and those remaining for the bonus to plan your work and reach the 2,000-delivery goal.
