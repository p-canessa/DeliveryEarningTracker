Side Menu

The Delivery Earning Tracker side menu allows you to navigate between the different sections of the app. To open it, tap the menu icon in the top left corner of the main screen.

Menu Items

  • Export Data: Allows you to export data in CSV format. For instructions, see the Data Export section.
  • Import Paystub: Allows you to upload your pay statements in PDF format. For instructions, see the Paystub Import section.
  • View Monthly Statement: Allows you to view your pay statements. For instructions, see the Statement Viewing section.
  • Quarterly Summary: Displays your earnings statistics on a quarterly basis. For details, see the Summaries section.
  • Annual Summary: Displays your earnings statistics on an annual basis. For details, see the Summaries section.
  • Paystub Reconciliation: Allows you to view differences between uploaded paystub data and orders entered in the database and reconcile them. For instructions, see the Paystub Reconciliation section.
  • Settings: Modify your app settings. For instructions, see the Settings section.
